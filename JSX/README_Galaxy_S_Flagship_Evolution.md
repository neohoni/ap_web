## 3. AP Strategy Evolution

| Generation | Base / Plus | Ultra | Strategic Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| S23 | Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 for Galaxy | Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 for Galaxy | Exynos risk removed, global performance consistency |
| S24 | Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 for Galaxy / Exynos 2400 by region | Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 for Galaxy | Regional AP split returns for Base / Plus; Ultra keeps Qualcomm premium track |
| S25 | Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy | Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy | Snapdragon-centered generation for AI performance |
| S26 | Exynos 2600 / Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy by region | Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy-centered | Exynos re-entry while Ultra stays Snapdragon-first |
| S27 Prediction | Exynos 2700 / Snapdragon Gen 6 by region | Snapdragon Gen 6 for Galaxy-first | Ultra likely remains Snapdragon due to performance, thermals, and brand trust |

### Key AP Insight

- **Base / Plus:** likely to be used for cost control and regional AP diversification.
- **Ultra:** likely to remain Snapdragon-first because it represents Samsung’s maximum performance, camera, AI, and benchmark credibility.
- **S27 Prediction:** Exynos 2700 may return more broadly, but Ultra-class devices are still expected to prioritize Snapdragon for Galaxy.

## 8. Recommended Scenario

### Recommended: Base Scenario

| Model | Recommended Direction | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **S27** | Exynos 2700 / Snapdragon Gen 6 by region + 4,500mAh + improved 50MP sensor | Base model should focus on price, battery, AI UX, and balanced performance |
| **S27+** | Exynos 2700 / Snapdragon Gen 6 by region + 5,000mAh + efficient QHD+ display | Plus should focus on large-screen productivity and battery life |
| **S27 Ultra** | Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 6 for Galaxy + 5,200mAh + large 200MP or 250MP-class camera + multimodal AI | Ultra should remain the maximum-performance camera / AI flagship |

### Why Base Scenario Is Recommended

1. **Realistic AP Strategy**  
   Base / Plus can use Exynos and Snapdragon by region, while Ultra keeps Snapdragon for Galaxy.

2. **Balanced Cost and Innovation**  
   Extreme hardware changes increase cost, heat, and thickness risk. Base Scenario gives meaningful upgrades without excessive risk.

3. **AI Differentiation**  
   S27’s real flagship value is likely to come from multimodal AI and personal assistant workflows rather than simple camera megapixel increases.

4. **Battery Improvement Needed**  
   S27 should break the long 5,000mAh Ultra plateau with a realistic 5,200mAh-class battery and faster charging.
